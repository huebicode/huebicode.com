An important and eye-opening documentary by Peter Porta explores the business model of Big Tech advertising companies like Google and Meta, and the associated rise of clickbait, disinformation, hate speech, and scams on the internet.

It explains why people like Musk and other shitpost accounts with millions of followers are justifying everything under the banner of “free speech” just to keep you glued to the screen.

The documentary is currently free to watch on arte.
