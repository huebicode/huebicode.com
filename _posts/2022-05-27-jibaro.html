--- layout: default title: Jibaro tags: [films] ---

Two hunters attracted against their nature by the fascination of the unattainable, allow closeness, wear themselves out against each other to succumb to their urges in the end.

An excellent 17 minute animation by Alberto Mielgo, whose work with "The Witness" already stood out with his distinctive style in the first season of the animation series "Love Death + Robots".
